ABOUT ME

My name is Maria Lara, and I am a trained jazz singer, actress and model. I have been performing and working for a number of years, and I enjoy every moment of it.

I have always had a passion for performing, and I hope to spread my love of jazz to a wider audience.

Please take a look below to find out more about what I do.

Singer

From an early age I have always wanted to sing. Whether by performing in the living room, or in loca talent shows, I have been passionate about singing.

I was trained by a singing coach from the age of 10 and found my love of jazz soon after. Jazz heavily influenced my life through University and it was then did I know that I wanted to continue and spread jazz on a bigger scale.

My singing heroes include: Frank Sinatra, Liza Minelli and Leonard Cohen.

Actress

Theatre has been one of my loves ever since I was a child and my dream is to perform on the West End.

I trained in Musical Theatre at the University of Essex and then spent a year with the American Musical Theatre School in London (AMTA) where I was able to travel and train in New York. It was at AMTA that I was able to showcase my work in an end of year show where I played the lead role. This was an amazing experience and only increased my love of theatre.

Model

I started modelling as a hobby for friend to work on their photography, and from that I found that I really enjoyed being infront of the camera.

Since then I have worked with a number of photoraphers in TF and paid shoots, which has enabled me to meet a lot of interesting people.

Being a model has significantly increased my confidence and how I feel about body image in general and I look forward to forward to having more shoots in future.
